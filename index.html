Aaron "Red Viper" Martell

Lord of Sunspear and Prince of Dorne

Aaron is the second son of Prince Lewyn Martell and his wife, Rhaeyna Targaryen, he came to be Prince of Dorne after his Lord father along with his first son Quentyn died fighting for the Targaryens in the sixth Blackfyre rebellion.
